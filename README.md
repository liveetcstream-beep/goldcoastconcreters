# Pimpama Rank & Rent Project

Welcome to your Rank & Rent project for **Pimpama, QLD, Australia**.

This folder contains the digital assets (websites, landing pages, tracking scripts) needed to rank local services on Google and rent them to Australian service providers.

---

## Niche: Concreting & Driveways 🏆

### Niche Setup & GMB Verification Rules
1. **Sister's Address is Perfect:** Yes! Your sister's residential address in Pimpama is your biggest asset. It will work perfectly for GMB verification.
2. **Privacy Protection (Service Area Business):** When setting up the Google Business Profile, **do not** display the physical address to the public. Set the business up as a **"Service Area Business"**. Specify "Pimpama QLD 4209" and surrounding suburbs (Coomera, Upper Coomera, Ormeau) as the service areas. Google will send the verification code postcard to your sister's house, but her address will remain hidden from the public.

## Scaling Strategy: The Hybrid Multi-Location Brand 🚀

To turn this into a **large-scale business** (covering Gold Coast, Brisbane, and beyond) rather than just a single suburb site, we use the **Hybrid Strategy**:

1. **Generic / Regional Domain Name:** Register a professional regional brand name (e.g. `coastlineconcreting.com.au` or `premierconcreting.com.au`).
2. **First Suburb Launch (Pimpama):** We start by building a dedicated page for Pimpama (e.g., `brand.com.au/pimpama`) and verifying the Google Business Profile as **"[Brand Name] - Pimpama"** using your sister's address. This gives us a fast SEO win due to hyper-local maps optimization.
3. **Multi-Location Expansion:** Once Pimpama ranks and generates passive rent, we duplicate the success by creating pages for Southport (`/southport`), Coomera (`/coomera`), and Brisbane (`/brisbane`), verifying GMBs there using friends' addresses or local office space services. We then rent out each suburb to a local contractor in that area.

## Multi-Location Call Routing & GBP Setup Secrets 🔑

### A. How Phone Numbers are Managed:
You do **not** need different physical SIM cards for each suburb.
1. **Option 1: Suburb-Specific VoIP Numbers (Recommended):**
   * Buy multiple local tracking numbers from a VoIP service (e.g. Zadarma, Crazytel) for $2–$4 AUD/month each.
   * Put **Number A** on the `/pimpama` page (forwards calls to Pimpama Contractor).
   * Put **Number B** on the `/southport` page (forwards calls to Southport Contractor).
2. **Option 2: Interactive Voice Response (IVR):**
   * Put one main Gold Coast number on the site.
   * Route calls via an automated menu: *"Press 1 for Northern GC (Pimpama), Press 2 for Central GC (Southport)."*

### B. How Addresses are Verified in Other Suburbs:
1. **Organic Landing Pages (No address needed):** You can rank `/southport` or `/brisbane` on Google Search Page 1 organically through content alone, generating phone calls without a Google Map listing.
2. **Friends & Family Network:** Pay friends/relatives in other suburbs a small fee ($100 one-off or 5% of monthly rent) to receive the Google verification postcard at their residential address (keeping it private on the map).
3. **Using the Renting Contractor's Address:** Once a contractor in a new suburb agrees to rent your lead flow from organic rankings, ask to verify a new Google Maps profile using their home/yard address. They will gladly agree since the calls are already theirs.
